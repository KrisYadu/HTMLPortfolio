The Best Movies According to Yadu

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Memories (Malayalam)

This is currently my favourite Malayalam movie. I love everything about it especially the screenplay and direction along with one of the finest background scoring. It's a splendid thriller!

Interstellar

One of the finest sci-fi movies ever. The more you watch the movie, the movie unviels itself on you. Christopher Nlan has did a fantastic job making this movie!

The Curious Case of Benjamin Button

Super beautiful film. The narration along the uniqness in the story makes it one of a kind movie. The lon-linear story telling in a scene is indeed a masterpiece. All in all, stellar cinematic treat.
